    The water from the two sources has different chemical-physical characteristics. However, both waters, due to their low fixed residue and limited hardness and ion content, can be classified as minimally mineralized waters, suitable for low-sodium diets and infant feeding. In particular, the water from the Rebruant source has a fixed residue of 22 mg/l and the Vinadio source has a sodium content of 0.9 mg/l.
