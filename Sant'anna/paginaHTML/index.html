  • Sant'Anna mineral water from Vinadio, or simply Sant'Anna water, is a mineral water that springs from two different sources located in the municipality of Vinadio, in the high Stura Valley, in the Piedmontese Maritime Alps, and is bottled and distributed by the company Fonti di Vinadio S.p.A. Fonti di Vinadio is a joint-stock company founded in 1996 by the Bertone family, previously active in the construction sector.
  • Alberto Bertone has held, since the foundation, and still holds, the positions of president and CEO. The commercial headquarters are located in Turin, while the legal headquarters and production plants are in the municipality of Vinadio. The company has grown very rapidly, and within about ten years (by 2007) the Sant'Anna brand reached leadership in the mineral water market, obtaining in 2009 a 12% market share in Italy.
