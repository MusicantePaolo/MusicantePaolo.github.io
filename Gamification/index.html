Gamification

What is Gamification?

Gamification, or "gamification", is an innovative strategy that applies elements typical of games in non-game contexts to increase interest, participation, and motivation of people.

Gamification, a term derived from the word "game", represents the application of game elements and mechanics in non-game contexts to stimulate the interest, participation, and interaction of users. This innovative strategy aims to transform everyday experiences, educational activities, or business processes, incorporating gaming elements to improve engagement and achieve specific goals.

The underlying idea of gamification is to exploit the natural human tendency for competition, gratification, and achievement, in order to positively influence people's behavior. Through the introduction of rewards, challenges, leaderboards, and other playful elements, gamification aims to make activities more engaging, increase motivation, and promote active participation.
